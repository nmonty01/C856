Traveling to the Island

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

Taniti Transportation

Island Ground Transportation
Public Bus

The public bus serves Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. You can reserve a public bus pass online, or purchase a pass at any bus stop in the city. You must have a pass to ride the bus.

Buy Pass
Private Bus

Renting a private bus is a great way to accommodate a large group! A private bus rental can take you anywhere on the island. Reservations are available by the hour, or by the day.

Taxi

Taxis are available within Taniti City. You can reserve a taxi online, or by phone. Gratuity is appreciated.

Rental Car

Rental cars are available from a local rental agency near the airport. View the website from the button below to see availability.

Bike & Helmet Rental

Bikes and helmts are available to rent from several vendors (helmets are required by law).

Walking

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing: this area is easy to explore on foot.
